Deliveries and Returns

Delivery Information

We offer free standard delivery on all orders. Orders are usually dispatched within 24 hours and delivered within 3-5 working days.

If you require express delivery, please contact us before placing your order.

Returns and Exchanges

If for any reason you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a 30-day returns policy from the date of delivery.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for return. We will provide you with a returns label and instructions for returning your item(s).

  • All items must be returned in their original condition with tags attached.
  • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 working days of receiving your returned item(s).
  • Original shipping fees are non-refundable.

Faulty Items

If you receive a faulty item, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your order. We will provide you with a returns label and instructions for returning your item(s).
